SereneStay

Discover a wonderful gem, a tranquil stay, surrounded by the serene azure waters of the Limuru waterfall. Relax and rejuvenate at a resort that stimulates and reinvigorates the soul and senses. In a resort unlike any other, an exquisite and worldly environment combines the finest levels of service with the quiet character of the Rift valley. A bustling resort and a magical retreat that combines excitement and relaxation, modernism and classical Kenyan culture with refined, eclectic gastronomy, and where a kind and inviting personnel will pamper you to create unforgettable memories.
