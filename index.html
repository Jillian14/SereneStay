SERENE STAY INLAND RESORT

Serene Stay hotel is located in Rift Valley in Loitoktok District, It is 30 minutes drive away from Amboseli Airport. Serena Stay hotel is positively one of the most peaceful and beautiful places in Rift Valley.

OUR FACILITIES

Take a look at what we offer and see what resonates with you.

SWIMMING POOL

The fascinating, open air swimming pool is located on an elevate terrace with a beautiful view. Swimming pool is constructed with cool blue tiles that enhance the color of water in the pool as it reflects the color of the sky.

THE CONFERENCE HALL

The conference hall is lit with natural light, the air-conditioned Conference hall is suitable for hosting about 200 people. It is equipped this up-to-date technology where high standard presentations can be held with built-in projector, DVD-CD player, available computers and surround system.

IN-BUILT PLAY ROOM

If your child shows a proclivity for art or if they simply have a lot of interest in it then this room is a great way for a child to get their creativity expressed. There are a number of different toys for them to play with and they can meet and make new friends and play!

OUR BEAUTIFUL LANDSCAPE FOR RELAXATION

Sit back, relax and enjoy the view. If you have been looking for a place where is green and wide enough for you to have a little picnic so as to escape from the concrete jungle then Serene Stay Inland Resort is the place to be!

OUR BREATHTAKING SPA

You can enjoy the run of the relaxing spa, a chance to get away from it all and chill out in peace and tranquillity and a delicious light bite style lunch while you’re there. Oh, and did we mention the refreshing express facial, manicure and deep tissue back massage that you’ll lose yourself in while you’re indulging?

ACTIVITIES OFFERED

There are a myriad of activities lined up for anyone who wants to have a good time while on vacation

NATURE TRAIL

Riding on trails is a very relaxing family event, especially when camping.

BIRD WATCHING

Bird watching, some call it birding, is a hobby where you stare and observe birds in the wild in their natural, home environment. People have been spotting, watching, and studying birds for hundreds of years. Trying to find out more about the many different types of birds we see today.

CAMPFIRE

It is the right time when the temperature drops down. For campfire night is the right time to celebrate with your friends and your loved ones.

ARCHERY

Archery is a precision sport where the competitors aim and shoot at a target using a bow and arrow.

ZIPLINING

Dare to challenge your fear as you let a rip and dive the steep hills using a zipline.
